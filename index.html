What is Saksharatha.ai Program by Entri?

The Saksharatha.ai Program by Entri is an introductory course designed to make Artificial Intelligence (AI) accessible and understandable to the general public. This course will be offered in multiple regional languages, including Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi, to ensure that learners from diverse linguistic backgrounds can benefit from it. The primary goal of this course is to provide a quick and easy learning experience about AI from a user’s perspective, enabling participants to incorporate AI tools into their daily lives to enhance productivity, efficiency, and overall quality of work.
